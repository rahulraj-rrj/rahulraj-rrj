## 🥂 About Me

I'm a backend-focused Computer Science undergraduate (**CGPA 8.6/10**, Galgotias University, 2022–2026) with hands-on experience architecting and shipping production-pattern systems in **Spring Boot** and **React**. My flagship project, **Encore**, is a concurrency-safe event ticket booking platform handling JWT auth, role-based authorization, and optimistic-locking seat reservation — deployed live across Docker, Render, and Netlify.

I care about systems that don't fall over under load, code that's boring in the best way, and shipping things end-to-end — from schema design to deployment.
